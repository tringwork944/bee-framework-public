# Mo dun giao_dien_mac_dinh

Mo dun giao dien mac dinh cua framework.

- Loai mo dun: `giao_dien`
- Mo dun loi, luon kich hoat, khong duoc tat/go cai dat.
- Cung cap layout, partial va tai nguyen UI.

## Cach tao giao dien moi
1. Copy `giao_dien_mac_dinh` thanh mo dun moi, vi du `giao_dien_toi`.
2. Sua `cau_hinh.php` (`ma`, `ten`, `phien_ban`, `loai = giao_dien`).
3. Chinh `giao_dien/bo_cuc`, `giao_dien/thanh_phan`, `tai_nguyen`.
4. Kich hoat mo dun giao dien moi trong quan ly mo dun.

Khi kich hoat giao dien moi, he thong tu tat giao dien cu va chi giu 1 giao dien active.
